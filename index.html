Why SplitView Is Blocked On Your TV

  • 1
    Kayo only enables SplitView on Apple TV, desktop web browsers, and mobile/tablet apps
  • 2
    Smart TVs (LG, Samsung, Hisense, Sony, etc.) are explicitly blocked — even if they have the Kayo app
  • 3
    Casting via Chromecast or AirPlay also disables SplitView — Kayo detects the cast session
  • 4
    The workarounds below bypass these restrictions using screen mirroring, HDMI, or UA spoofing
